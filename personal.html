How are we going to implement these ideas in Belding

Implementing Bikes in Belding

Public bikes are the main transportation update the city will be able to implement. We took the price of $200 each and we thought 50 bikes spread across 10 locations would be an adequate amount.

Belding has a small downtown area that is easy to navigate on a bicycle and also a highway that has much of the population and businesses on it, so bikes are an ideal mode of transportation.

Each night we would pay a person for collecting the bikes and replacing them in their original racks for the beginning of the day. The bikes can be repaired and maintained at the bike shop in Greenville and any simple repairs can be done in house.

Implementing Scooters in Belding

As well as we are going to do with the bikes we are going to implement around 50 scooters all over the main areas in Belding.

We are going to contact companies as Lime to give as the scooters and just with their mobile application they woould be able to rent the scooters and after that pay the amount of time that they have been riding the scooter.

The average cost for a scooter is about $300 and the maintenance is depending on the damage, so it ranges is $50-$200.
