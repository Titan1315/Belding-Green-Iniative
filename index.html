Keep Belding Green!

Reducing our town's carbon footprint

Our goal is to study the best ways to reduce the carbon footprint in the Belding area, and in order to achieve this goal we had done a research about 4 main ways to help the environment of our town.

These main ways are: Individual Transportation, Energy efficiency, Public Transportation and Recycling. In the individual transportation section we’ve looked about vehicles that could be included in the town and that may reduce the carbon footprint, as well as the public transportation where we’ve looked for the better ways to implement buses and taxis all over Belding.

On the other hand we have the Energy Efficiency and Recycling, which are two good ways to reduce carbon footprint in Belding because the city doesn’t have either a good recycling method or a good energy efficiency and we have discovered how to improve these two elements to make Belding a little bit more green!
