Recycling project to reduce the carbon footprint in Belding

Each year the U.S produces 292.4 million tons of trash, and as of 2019 the population of Belding was 5,718 meaning that with an average of 62 tons of trash per person was produced, on average Belding produces 354,516 tons of trash each year.

This information is frightening because with a simple plan of action we would be able to reduce the amount of trash that Belding’s residents produce by a massive number all just by recycling. The problem is that Belding doesn't have a recycling program. Which needed a solution, one that we planned.

Implementing a Recycling Program in Belding

Implementing a recycling program in Belding is not really hard and it would reduce most of the trash that Belding produces. But we would have to follow a plan of action:

Action Plan

1º Contact Near Cities

Many cities near Belding already have a recycling program, so the easiest way to implement recycling in our city would be to contact the recycling companies near Belding and reach out to them.

2º Expand their Routes

The next step would be to ask these companies to expand their recycling routes so they go through Belding, home to home taking the recycling that people would have in their respective container outside their houses.

3º Decide what to Recycle

We would have to decide what materials are we going to recycle in Belding. They are going to be: paper, plastic, glass, cans and organic items; each material would have its own container so people could throw the trash in the appropiated container.

4º Let People Know

The final and hardest step is to convince people and let them know that a new recycle program is running on Belding so they would buy new containers and start to recycle. We would do it by sending letters to all of Beldings citizens, hanging flyers around the town and by making advertisements

Results of Implementing Recycling in Belding

Each year the U.S on average 31% of all waste is recycled this means that since belding has about 5,718 people and produces around 354,516 tons of trash each year we could recycle upwards of

100,000 tons of trash in Belding alone.

How does recycling reduce carbon footprint?

Recycling helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions by reducing energy consumption using recycled materials to make new products reduces the need for raw materials manufacturing products from recycled material typically requires less energy than making products from virgin materials.

A recycling program would not only help all of Belding but it would give members of the community a feeling of contributing. Starting a recycling program is a somewaht easy task and would be extremely benifical to all people and would lower the carbon footprint of Belding
