Public transportation Ideas to reduce the carbon footprint in Belding

Public tranpostation is the main method to reduce the carbon footprint in any city in the US. Transportation is one of the main sources of C02 in the planet and in order to reduce that we should have and use more public transportaion.

Due to that we have come with some ideas abouot implementing public buses, taxis and gondolas all over Belding so we can make Belding cleaner. But implementing these ideas isn't cheap, so we will have to ether raise a lot of money or the Belding governor should start thinking about using more money to invest it into the public transportation.

Public transportation ideas

Our main ideas is to implement public buses and taxis with bus and taxi stops in the main areas of Belding such as the high school, the Wesco gas station or the skatepark. In addition we thought about the idea of implementing gondolas, a modern type of transportation that uses cable car to move you to different parts of the city.

Public Buses

Implementing public buses might be the idea that requires the most money because each bus would cost around $250 000 with a maintenance of $35 000 a year, but if we include these type of transportation in the main areas and near most of the houses in belding (so you just have to walk 5 minutes to each bus stop), we would reduce the carbon footprint a lot.

Buses
Public buses and stops over the main areas of Belding

Taxi
Implementing a public taxi service with new taxi drivers jobs

Public Taxis

Adding public taxis not only is going to reduce the carbon footprint but it is going to create new jobs and the economy will be benefit. Adding a public taxis service with around 20 taxis in all belding and each car costing around $10 000 and a maintenance of $5 000 a year, it will cost around $250 000.

Public Gondolas

Gondolas are a brand new system of transportaion that can revolutionize the way that we move. It could be used as a touristic way of moving trough Belding or as a transportation method to go to work. Including around 4 main stations where the gondolas will be moving all day long will cost around $45 000 and with a maintenance of $152 000 a year because it is a new transportation method and it could have some problems.

Gondola
Implementing 4 public gondola stations that works 24 hours a day
Initial Cost:
$45 000

Areas to implement the public transportation ideas in Belding

Once we have decided the main ideas that we are going to introduce in Belding to improve the public transportaiton and to reduce the carbon footprint we have to decide where are we going to implement them, because, in order to be effective we have to choose wisely the areas where we are going to implement the bus and taxi stops and the gondola stations.

There are many locations where we can add them but we have selected the ones that would be more effective because more people visit those places.

Wesco

Wesco is the main gas station in Belding and everyone goes there at least once a day, so it would be a great idea to add here bus and taxi stops and a gondola station, because it will be a center area in Belding where people would be able to move aorund easily. And after arriving to Wesco using the bus they can use the personal tranportation like bikes or scooters to keep moving around.

BHS

Belding High School has already a public bus system with the kids that are in there, but, what if we added a gondola station right next to it? Many people go to the Belding High school no tonly to attend to school but also to go to the football or volleyball games, so adding a gondola station that connects with the high school would be a great idea.

Leppinks

Many people buy their groceries at Leppinks, so having public transportation in this area of Belding would benefit a lot of people and they would prefer using these transportation methods rather than their cars.

Skatepark

The skatepark area is a place where many teenagers hangout, not only because of the skatepark but because of the many things that are sourrounding the area such as the soccer field or the museum.
