Public transportation Ideas to reduce the carbon footprint in Belding

Public tranpostation is the main method to reduce the carbon footprint in any city in the US. Transportation is one of the main sources of C02 in the planet and in order to reduce that we should have and use more public transportaion.

Due to this we have come up with some ideas about implementing public buses, taxis and gondolas all over Belding so we can make Belding cleaner. But implementing these ideas isn't cheap, so we will have to either raise money or the Belding mayor should start thinking about using more money to invest it into the public transportation.

Public transportation ideas

Our main ideas are implement public buses and taxis with bus and taxi stops in the main areas of Belding such as the high school, Wesco gas station or the skatepark. In addition we thought about the idea of implementing gondolas, a modern type of transportation system that uses cable cars to move you to different parts of the city.

Public Buses

Implementing public buses might be the idea that requires the most money because each bus would cost around $250 000 with a maintenance cost of $35 000 a year, but if we include these types of transportation systems in the main areas and most of the houses in belding (so you just have to walk 5 minutes to each bus stop), we would reduce the carbon footprint greatly.

Public Taxis

Adding public taxis is not only going to reduce the carbon footprint but it is going to create new jobs and the economy will be benefit.

Adding a public taxi service with around 20 taxis in all of Belding, each car will end up costing around $10 000 and with a maintenance of $5 000 a year, it will cost around $250 000.

Public Gondolas

Gondolas are a brand new system of transportaion that can revolutionize the way we move. It could be used as a touristic way of moving trough Belding or as a transportation method to go to work.

This system will end up including around 4 main stations where the gondolas will be moving all day long will cost around $45 000 and with a maintenance of $152 000 a year this is because it is a new type of transportation and it could have some problems.

Gondola
Implementing 4 public gondola stations that works 24 hours a day
Initial Cost:
$45 000

Areas to implement the public transportation ideas in Belding

Wesco Gas Station

1 / 4

Wesco is the main gas station in Belding and most people go there at least once a day, so we feel it would be a great idea to add a bus, taxi and gondola stations here, because it is a cental area in Belding where people would be able to move around more smoothly. After arriving at Wesco using a bus they can use the personal tranportation like bikes or scooters to keep moving around.

Belding High School

2 / 4

Belding High School has already a public bus system with the kids that are atending the school, but, what if we added a gondola station right next to it? Many people go to Belding High school not only to attend school but also to go to the football or other sport games, so adding a gondola station that connects with the high school would be a great idea.

Leppinks

3 / 4

Many people buy their groceries at Leppinks, having public transportation in this area of Belding would benefit a huge group of people and they would prefer using these transportation systems rather than their cars.

Skatepark

4 / 4

The skatepark is a place where many teenagers hangout, not only because of the skatepark but because of the many things that are sourrounding the area such as the soccer field, park and or the museum.
